# SOFT-DRINK-MARKET-INSIGHT-CHALLENGE-BY-OLUWATOSIN-MOSES
The Soft Drink Market Insight Challenge is a data visualization competition focused on analyzing real-world survey data from Alimosho Local Government Area, Lagos, Nigeria. I hereby explore soft drink availability, packaging, outlet types, and brand presence to uncover insights into market distribution, consumer trends, and brand dominance.
## Soft Drink Market Analysis — Alimosho LGA, Lagos, Nigeria
### Project Overview
This project explores soft drink availability, packaging, outlet types, and brand presence in Alimosho Local Government Area, Lagos, Nigeria.
The goal is to uncover insights into market distribution, consumer trends, and brand dominance using real-world survey data.
### Objectives
- Understand the structure and quality of the dataset
- Analyze numerical and categorical variables
- Explore soft drink packaging types and availability
- Identify outlet types selling soft drinks
- Examine brand presence and dominance in the market
